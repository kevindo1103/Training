## Phần 2: BD Process & Pipeline

### 2.1 BD Funnel cho IT Services Company

```
Stage 1: AWARENESS & LEAD GENERATION
├── Inbound: Content marketing, SEO, events, webinars
├── Outbound: Cold outreach, LinkedIn, referrals
├── Partners: Technology partners, consulting firms
└── KPI: # qualified leads / month

Stage 2: QUALIFICATION
├── BANT check (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
├── Technical fit assessment
├── Commercial viability check
└── KPI: Qualification rate (% leads → qualified)

Stage 3: DISCOVERY & SOLUTION DESIGN
├── Technical deep-dive với prospect
├── Understand pain points & success criteria
├── Design proposed solution & team
└── KPI: Proposal rate (% qualified → proposal)

Stage 4: PROPOSAL & NEGOTIATION
├── Technical proposal + commercial proposal
├── Proof of Concept (nếu cần)
├── Contract negotiation
└── KPI: Win rate (% proposals → signed)

Stage 5: ONBOARDING & DELIVERY
├── Kickoff & knowledge transfer
├── Deliver value nhanh (quick wins)
├── Upsell & cross-sell opportunities
└── KPI: Time-to-value, NPS, expansion rate
```

### 2.2 Pipeline Management Framework

**Pipeline Stages & Probability:**

| Stage | Probability | Actions Required | Max Time in Stage |
|-------|------------|------------------|-------------------|
| Lead | 10% | Qualify BANT, schedule intro call | 2 weeks |
| Qualified | 20% | Discovery call, technical assessment | 3 weeks |
| Discovery | 40% | Solution design, proposal prep | 4 weeks |
| Proposal | 60% | Submit proposal, handle objections | 3 weeks |
| Negotiation | 80% | Contract terms, pricing finalization | 4 weeks |
| Closed Won | 100% | Handoff to delivery | - |

**Pipeline Health Metrics:**

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Pipeline Coverage | Pipeline value / Revenue target | ≥ 3x |
| Pipeline Velocity | # deals × win rate × avg deal size / cycle time | Increasing trend |
| Average Deal Size | Total pipeline / # deals | Increasing |
| Sales Cycle Length | Avg days from lead to close | Decreasing |
| Win Rate | Closed won / Total proposals | ≥ 25% |

### 2.3 Lead Generation Channels

**Hiệu quả các kênh cho IT Services Company tại VN:**

| Channel | Cost | Quality | Volume | Best For |
|---------|------|---------|--------|----------|
| **Referrals** | Low | Very High | Low | Enterprise deals |
| **LinkedIn (outbound)** | Medium | Medium | Medium | Targeted accounts |
| **Technology Partners** | Medium | High | Medium | Access to partner's clients |

| **Clutch/GoodFirms** | Medium | Medium | Medium | SMB clients |
| **Direct outreach (email)** | Low | Low | High | Volume play |
| **Broker/Intermediary** | High | Variable | Low | Large deals |

## Phần 3: Sales Playbook

### 3.1 Discovery Framework (MEDDPICC cho IT Services)

Áp dụng cho mỗi deal:

| Element | Câu hỏi | Đánh giá |
|---------|---------|----------|
| **M**etrics | KPIs nào prospect muốn cải thiện? | |
| **E**conomic Buyer | Ai ký check? Ai có budget authority? | |
| **D**ecision Criteria | Họ đánh giá vendors bằng tiêu chí gì? | |
| **D**ecision Process | Quy trình mua hàng như thế nào? Bao nhiêu vòng? | |
| **P**aper Process | Legal/procurement process? Contract requirements? | |
| **I**dentify Pain | Pain point chính? Impact nếu không giải quyết? | |
| **C**hampion | Ai trong org ủng hộ Dolphin? | |
| **C**ompetition | Ai là đối thủ trong deal này? | |

### 3.2 Proposal Structure

**Cấu trúc proposal chuẩn cho IT Services:**

```
1. Executive Summary (1 trang)
   → Tóm tắt hiểu biết về vấn đề + solution tổng quan

2. Understanding of Requirements (2-3 trang)
   → Chứng minh Dolphin hiểu sâu business need

3. Proposed Solution (3-5 trang)
   → Architecture, technology stack, approach
   
4. Team & Methodology (2-3 trang)
   → Team profile, delivery methodology, tools
   
5. Project Plan (1-2 trang)
   → Timeline, milestones, deliverables
   
6. Commercial Proposal (1-2 trang)
   → Pricing, payment terms, assumptions
   
7. Why Dolphin (1 trang)
   → Differentiators, relevant experience, references
   
8. Appendices
   → Case studies, certifications, company profile
```

### 3.3 Objection Handling

**Top objections trong IT services sales và cách xử lý:**

| Objection | Root Cause | Response Strategy |
|-----------|-----------|-------------------|
| "Giá cao hơn đối thủ" | Chưa thấy value | Reframe: TCO, risk, quality |
| "Chưa có kinh nghiệm domain" | Trust gap | Show transferable experience + pilot |
| "Công ty nhỏ, lo về scale" | Risk concern | Reference clients, growth plan, backup plan |
| "Đang dùng vendor khác rồi" | Switching cost | Start small, parallel run, prove value |
| "Cần suy nghĩ thêm" | Unclear urgency | Quantify cost of delay |
| "Cần nói chuyện với team" | Multi-stakeholder | Offer to present to broader team |

## Phần 4: Account Management & Growth

### 4.1 Account Tiering

| Tier | Criteria | Approach | BD Allocation |
|------|----------|----------|---------------|
| **Strategic** (A) | Revenue > X, Growth potential high | Dedicated Account Manager, QBR, exec alignment | 50% time |
| **Growth** (B) | Revenue moderate, Growth potential high | Regular touchpoints, upsell focus | 30% time |
| **Maintain** (C) | Revenue stable, Limited growth | Reactive support, operational excellence | 15% time |
| **Evaluate** (D) | Low revenue, Low margin | Consider phase-out or transform | 5% time |

### 4.2 Account Growth Playbook

**"Land & Expand" Strategy:**

```
LAND (Tháng 1-3)
├── Win 1 project/team nhỏ
├── Deliver exceptional quality
├── Build relationships beyond buyer
└── Document success metrics

EXPAND (Tháng 4-12)  
├── Propose additional services/teams
├── Introduce to other departments
├── Share insights & thought leadership
├── Become "trusted advisor"

TRANSFORM (Year 2+)
├── Move from vendor → partner status
├── Joint innovation / co-creation
├── Strategic roadmap alignment
├── Executive sponsorship both sides
```

### 4.3 QBR (Quarterly Business Review) Template

```
1. Performance Summary
   • Delivered vs committed
   • Quality metrics (bugs, SLA, satisfaction)
   • Team performance & stability

2. Business Impact
   • How Dolphin's work impacted client's KPIs
   • ROI calculation

3. Insights & Recommendations
   • Observations about client's tech/business
   • Proactive suggestions for improvement

4. Roadmap & Next Steps
   • Upcoming opportunities
   • Team/capability investments
   • Strategic alignment discussion
```

## Phần 5: BD Team Structure & KPIs

### 5.1 BD Team Structure (Gợi ý cho company 200-500 người)

```
Head of BD / VP Sales
├── BD Manager (New Business)
│   ├── BD Representative (Market A - e.g., Japan)
│   ├── BD Representative (Market B - e.g., US/EU)
│   └── BD Representative (Market C - e.g., Domestic)
├── Account Manager (Existing Accounts)
│   ├── Account Manager (Strategic Accounts)
│   └── Account Manager (Growth Accounts)
├── Pre-Sales / Solution Architect
│   └── Supports both new & existing
└── Marketing (nếu chưa có team riêng)
    ├── Content & Events
    └── Digital Marketing
```
